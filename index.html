For Women By Women

SheRyds is a 100% Electric vehicle ride sharing Mobility as a Service (MaaS), driven by women for women ensures safe, secure, reliable, comfortable and green ride always.

Reducing Emissions Together

SheRyds embraces the responsibility to improve the quality of air, we breathe, of the city we love so much. Contribute by riding with SheRyds to help us plant trees across the city and improve the AQI and reduce emissions.

Empowering Women

SheRyds is not just a ride sharing platform, it is a revolution to empower poor and deprived women by offering an opportunity to learn, earn and sustain and bring themselves into mainstream of the economy.
