About Us

SHE RYDS aims to offer green, affordable, safe and comfortable Scooty Taxi Service for every metro passenger with an all-electric vehicle fleet. Hence improving air quality and making our city cleaner.

SHE RYDS is another initiative that will transform the way women travel. SHE RYDS empowers women to be independent, self-reliant, confident and comfortable in travelling from one destination to another. SHE RYDS empowers women and protects our beloved city’s environment by reducing carbon emissions through its E-Scooty Taxi Service.

SHE RYDS also empowers the “She She Rydss” by offering employment opportunities to women and giving them an opportunity to come into the mainstream, become bread earners for themselves and their families, earn with dignity and live with respect.

For every ride taken, we will plant a tree. This not only will help in offsetting any minimal emissions produced but also contributes to reforestation efforts and biodiversity.

SHE RYDS is a technologically driven company with the aim of transforming the landscape of Mobility-based transit into Mobility as a Service(MaaS) in the mass transit industry
