Customers Safety

By leveraging the latest safety technologies and onboarding / training process for our SheRydrs.
Every ride is tracked by SheRyds, with access to exact location of the rydr

SheRydr Verification

Our customers safety is of prime importance to us and to ensure each customers safety, security and comfort we conduct stringent background verification of She Rydrs before onboarding on our platform.

App Safety Features

Trip Sharing

Female Passengers can share their trip status with their respective emergency contacts on SheRyds App after the trip starts

Information Masking

Female Passengers mobile number is masked to protect their privacy and interaction with SheRydr happens only through App.

QRT : Quick Reaction Team

In case of an emergency our QRT swings into action and connects the customer to the local PCR and reach the location to ensure customer safety.

Customer Support

Customer and SheRydr can report any kind of incidence or issues through the 24*7 support feature on the app post & during the ride.

Two-way Rating System

Post the ride, Customer and SheRydr can give ratings to each other and any rating below 3 is flagged by us. SheRyds reaches out to them in 10 minutes to address their concern. The SheRydr is terminated immediately in case of any misconduct.

SOS Button

SOS button is a 24*7 support feature in the App that connects the customer with the law enforcement agency, she Ryds Support Team and Quick Reaction Team to help the customer in case of an emergency.

SheRydr Information

Once a ride is booked by, following details of SheRydrs are sent to the female passengers

  1. She Rydrs Name
  2. Vehicle Number
  3. Vehicle Location
  4. Star Rating of She Rydr
