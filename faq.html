FaQ's

The SheRyds app can be downloaded by customers either from the Google Play Store or directly from the website to book a ride with SheRyds.

Once you have downloaded the app, you will have to complete the signup process and then you can request a ride by entering your pickup and drop-off locations.

SheRyds operates from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily with its focus on providing safe, affordable and last-mile connectivity for women in Delhi.

SheRyds does not implement peak or surge pricing, meaning you can expect consistent, transparent pricing for your rides. Our flat pricing policy ensures affordability and fairness for all our passengers.

SheRyds's unique approach is offering female passengers rides with female drivers and specialized pick-up/drop-off locations, and using 100% Electric Vehicles for safety, comfort, and environmental consciousness.
