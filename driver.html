SheRydrs Safety

All our She Rydrs are well trained and are equipped to handle themselves with poise in case of any emergency and otherwise. All Rides are insured offering safety and security to our She Rydrs

SheRydr App
Insurance

Insurance can be claimed for any accident that occurs during the ride covering OPD charges, hospitalization and accidental benefit.

Customer Support

Customer and She Rydr can report any kind of incidence or issues through the 24*7 support feature on the app post & during the ride.

QRT : Quick Reaction Team

In case of an emergency our QRT swings into action and connects the customer to the local PCR and reach the location to ensure customer safety.

Two-way Rating System

Post the ride, Customer and She Rydr can give ratings to each other and any rating below 3 is flagged by us. Rydr reaches out to them in 10 minutes to address their concern. The Driver is terminated immediately in case of any misconduct.
